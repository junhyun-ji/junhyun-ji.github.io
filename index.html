Hi! I'm Jun Hyun Ji (first name pronounced as ``Joon-hyeon''), a 4th-year economics Ph.D. student at University of Pittsburgh. I major in microeconomic theory. My research interest lies in economic contexts in which preferences for non-conventional objects (e.g., beliefs, menus, and preferences) influence our behavior and social interactions.

A Representation of Preference over Preferences and the Act of Choosing, December 2024 PDF
